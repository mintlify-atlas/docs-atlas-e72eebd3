## What is upLegal?

upLegal is Chile's leading legal marketplace platform that connects clients with verified lawyers across all specialties. Whether you need representation for family law, labor disputes, real estate matters, or any legal service, upLegal makes it simple to find, book, and pay for legal consultations online.

## Key features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Verified lawyer profiles" icon="shield-check">
    All lawyers on upLegal are verified through the Poder Judicial de Chile (PJUD) system. Each lawyer's RUT and credentials are authenticated to ensure you're working with legitimate legal professionals.
    
    - Real-time verification with PJUD registry
    - Profile completion tracking
    - Public reviews and ratings
    - Years of experience displayed
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Smart search and filtering" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Find the perfect lawyer for your needs using our advanced search system:
    
    - Search by specialty (19+ legal areas)
    - Filter by location across all Chilean regions
    - Price range filtering
    - Minimum rating and experience filters
    - Real-time availability checking
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Online booking system" icon="calendar">
    Book consultations directly through the platform with our intuitive scheduling:
    
    - View lawyer availability in real-time
    - Select consultation duration (30, 60, 90, or 120 minutes)
    - Choose between video call or phone consultation
    - Automatic slot reservation during checkout
    - Integration with lawyer's Google Calendar
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Secure payments" icon="credit-card">
    Process payments securely through MercadoPago integration:
    
    - Multiple payment methods supported
    - Automatic payment confirmation
    - 10% platform service fee clearly displayed
    - Instant booking confirmation emails
    - Payment tracking in user dashboard
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Dashboard management" icon="chart-line">
    Both clients and lawyers get dedicated dashboards:
    
    **For clients:**
    - View upcoming and past consultations
    - Manage favorite lawyers
    - Track payment history
    - Update profile settings
    
    **For lawyers:**
    - Manage appointments and consultations
    - Set custom availability schedules
    - Track earnings and analytics
    - Configure profile and services
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up as a client or lawyer with email verification. Lawyers undergo RUT verification through PJUD.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for lawyers or clients">
    Clients search and filter lawyers by specialty, location, and price. Lawyers receive booking requests from clients.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book a consultation">
    Select available time slots, choose consultation duration, and complete secure payment through MercadoPago.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attend your consultation">
    Receive video call or phone consultation details. Lawyers and clients connect at scheduled time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Leave a review">
    After the consultation, clients can rate and review their experience to help other users.
  </Step>
</Steps>
